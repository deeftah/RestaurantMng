Business Requirements Document
ICS 499 Term Project Requirements
Restaurant Management Software

Author: Tyler Thrash, Ann Putnam (Monteith)
Date: 01/21/18

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Overview:

This Requirements Document will provide the requirements for a Restaurant 
Management software. Both functional and non-functional requirement will be 
documented, as well as stakeholders.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Stakeholders (Those affected by the application. Business users who will be 
using this system): 

Restaurant owners, employees of the restaurant that uses
this software.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Functional Requirements (feature specific):

1. Employees must be able login to the system and the system shall recognize 
   their location and their personell information in relation to the business.
   
2. The system must allow for the entry and updating of menu items that are 
   currently available at the restaurant.
   
3. The system must allow for customer reservations, including the number of
   people in the party and the reservation name.
   
4. The system must allow for the placement of customer orders.

5. The system must track what customers have ordered and the total amount that
   the customer owes to the restaurant.
   
6. The system must allow for multiple payment options, such as cash, check, and
   credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover, or American Express).
   
7. The system must accept the payment method provided by the customer and 
   process it appropriately.

8. The system must track the amount of tips received as a part of the employee's
   wages.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
